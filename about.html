About Me

Bioinformatician | Genomic Researcher | Data Scientist

I am a dedicated researcher with expertise in bioinformatics, computational genomics, and antimicrobial resistance. My work focuses on applying computational approaches to understand microbial diversity, track antimicrobial resistance, and develop predictive models for public health applications.

Research Philosophy

My research integrates wet lab techniques with computational analysis to address pressing public health challenges. I believe in open science and collaborative research that bridges the gap between computational biology and laboratory science.

Career Goals

To establish a research program that leverages genomic technologies and data science to combat antimicrobial resistance in low-resource settings. I aim to develop accessible bioinformatics tools and training programs to build capacity in computational biology across Africa.
